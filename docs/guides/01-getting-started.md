# 1. Getting started

*Audience: everyone.*

## Opening DataForms

Click **DataForms** in the Nextcloud top bar (the app icons next to the search).
You must be logged in; there are no public links.

## What you see

- **Left sidebar** — your registers. **Favourites** appear at the top (star a
  register to pin it), then **All registers**. Each has a small colour dot and a
  record count.
- **Main area** — when no register is selected, a **dashboard** of register cards.
  Click one to open it.

## Opening a register

Click a register in the sidebar or a card on the dashboard. You land on its
**Records** tab — the table of everything in that register.

Across the top of a register you'll see **tabs**:

- **Records** — everyone sees this.
- **Fields · Forms · Rules · Automations** — only **managers** see these (they're
  for *designing* the register). If you don't see them, you have viewer or
  contributor access, which is normal.

## Header buttons

- **Copy link** — copies a direct link to this register (and the open tab). Paste
  it into Talk, an email, or a wiki page; whoever opens it must be logged in and
  have access.
- **Share** — *(managers)* give other people access. See
  [Managing registers → Sharing](03-managing-registers.md#sharing--permissions).

## Creating a register

Click **+ New register** (top of the sidebar), give it a **title**, an optional
**description** and a **colour**, then **Create**. You're now the register's
**manager**. Next: [add fields](03-managing-registers.md#fields).

## Tips

- The app remembers nothing sensitive in your browser — everything lives on the
  Nextcloud server.
- After an update you might see an old screen; press **Ctrl+F5** to refresh.
